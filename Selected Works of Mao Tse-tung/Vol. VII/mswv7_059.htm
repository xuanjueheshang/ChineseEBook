Telegram to President Prasad of the Republic of India

January 28, 1950

[ Source: People's Daily, January 29, 1950.]

President Rajendra Prasad

The Republic of India

 

Your Excellency:

At this time, on Your Excellency's election as the first President of India, I express on behalf of the Central People's Government and the people of the People's Republic of China, our sincere congratulations.

 

(Signed as Chairman of CPG of PRC and dated)
